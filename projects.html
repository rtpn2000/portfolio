DVPO StaG

    DVPO StaG is a chatbot system designed to support survivors of domestic violence in Washington State by helping them file for Domestic Violence Protection Orders (DVPOs). The platform focuses on legal precision, and accessibility, ensuring that survivors feel heard, supported, and guided throughout a difficult legal process.

  • Chatbot built on Meta LLaMA 3.1, to conduct structured yet conversational interviews with survivors to gather legally relevant information.
  • Integrated a RAG pipeline with FAISS indexing to provide context-aware, legally accurate responses.
  • Automatically generates legally-sound survivor statements based on user responses. These statements are designed to reduce legal ambiguity and increase the likelihood of petition approval.

When To Take Your Disney Vacay?

    "When To Take Your Disney Vacay" is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to help users determine the optimal time to visit Disney parks. By combining ride wait times, weather data, and visitor sentiment, the dashboard provides a holistic view of park conditions to enhance planning and overall experience.

  • Wait Time Dashboard: Displays hourly ride wait times and a dynamic list of rides with the shortest waits, allowing users to plan their day efficiently.
  • Weather Dashboard: Shows hourly temperatures and the chance of rain for selected days, helping visitors choose comfortable and dry periods for their visit.
  • Emotion Dashboard: Visualizes visitor sentiment using word clouds from best and worst reviews and emotion-based bar charts, highlighting positive and negative experiences during selected dates.
  • Date Range Interaction: Users can select a date range that updates all dashboards simultaneously, providing a synchronized and dynamic planning experience.
